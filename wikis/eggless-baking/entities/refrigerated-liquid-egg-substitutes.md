# Refrigerated Liquid Egg Substitutes

Refrigerated liquid egg substitutes are typically sold in cartons and are designed to be used as a direct 1:1 replacement for eggs in cooking and baking. These substitutes offer a convenient option for those looking to avoid traditional eggs while still achieving similar results in their recipes.

For more information on various egg substitutes, see [[Egg Substitutes Comparison]].

## Contradictions

- **serious-eats-egg-substitutes-tested.md:** The existing page states that refrigerated liquid egg substitutes are designed to be a direct 1:1 replacement for eggs, implying they can effectively replicate the functions of eggs in recipes. However, the new claim asserts that no single substitute can replicate all the roles that eggs perform in baking, indicating a limitation in the effectiveness of these substitutes.
